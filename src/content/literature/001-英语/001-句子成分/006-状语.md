# 状语

状语修饰动词、形容词、副词或整个句子，说明时间、地点、原因、方式等。

## 基本定义

- 状语位置灵活，可放在句首、句中或句尾
- 可以由单词、短语或从句充当
- 说明"什么时候"、"在哪里"、"怎样"、"为什么"等

## 例句

1. He runs <mark>**quickly**</mark>.
   （他跑得很快。）

2. <mark>**Yesterday**</mark>, I went to school.
   （昨天，我去上学了。）

3. She studies English <mark>**every day**</mark>.
   （她每天学英语。）

4. He is tall <mark>**because of his parents**</mark>.
   （他个子高是因为他的父母。）

5. <mark>**Although it rained**</mark>, we still went out.
   （虽然下雨了，我们还是出去了。）

## 状语的类型

| 类型 | 常见连词/词组 | 例句 |
|------|---------------|------|
| 时间状语 | yesterday, now, in 2020 | I saw him <mark>**yesterday**</mark>. |
| 地点状语 | here, in Beijing, at school | She lives <mark>**in Beijing**</mark>. |
| 原因状语 | because, since, because of | He was absent <mark>**because he was sick**</mark>. |
| 目的状语 | to, in order to, so that | I study <mark>**to learn**</mark>. |
| 结果状语 | so...that, too...to | He was so tired <mark>**that he slept**</mark>. |
| 方式状语 | quickly, with joy | She spoke <mark>**softly**</mark>. |
| 条件状语 | if, unless, whether | <mark>**If it rains**</mark>, we will stay home. |
| 让步状语 | although, though, even if | <mark>**Although it was cold**</mark>, he went out. |
| 程度状语 | very, quite, extremely | He is <mark>**very**</mark> tall. |

## 状语的位置

### 句首
放在句首通常为了强调。

> <mark>**In the morning**</mark>, I drink coffee.
> （早上，我喝咖啡。）

### 句中
修饰动词时常用。

> He <mark>**always**</mark> comes late.（他总是迟到。）

### 句尾
最常见的位置。

> I study English <mark>**at home**</mark>.（我在家学英语。）

## 状语的成分

| 类型 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 副词 | He runs <mark>**fast**</mark>. |
| 介词短语 | She is in the room. |
| 分词短语 | <mark>**Seeing the teacher**</mark>, he ran away. |
| 不定式 | I came <mark>**to help you**</mark>. |
| 状语从句 | I will go <mark>**if you go**</mark>. |
