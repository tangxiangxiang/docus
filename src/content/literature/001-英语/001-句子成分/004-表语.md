# 表语

表语说明主语的身份、特征或状态，位于系动词之后。

## 基本定义

- 表语位于连系动词（be, become, get, seem 等）之后
- 说明主语"是什么"或"怎么样"
- 与主语形成主系表结构

## 例句

1. She is <mark>**a teacher**</mark>.
   （她是一名老师。）

2. The flower is <mark>**beautiful**</mark>.
   （这朵花很美。）

3. He seems <mark>**tired**</mark>.
   （他看起来很累。）

4. The problem is <mark>**easy**</mark>.
   （这道题很简单。）

5. My dream is <mark>**to become a doctor**</mark>.
   （我的梦想是成为一名医生。）

## 表语的成分

| 类型 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 名词 | He is <mark>**a student**</mark>. |
| 形容词 | She is <mark>**happy**</mark>. |
| 介词短语 | The book is <mark>**on the desk**</mark>. |
| 动名词 | His job is <mark>**teaching**</mark>. |
| 不定式 | Her wish is <mark>**to travel**</mark>. |
| 从句 | The question is <mark>**whether he comes**</mark>. |

## 常见系动词

| 类型 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| be 动词 | is, am, are, was, were |
| 状态变化 | become, get, turn, grow |
| 感官动词 | look, sound, smell, taste, feel |
| 保持状态 | remain, stay, keep |
